## Plan Changes

### Upgrade Your Plan
<Steps>
  1. Select the new plan you want to upgrade to
  2. Review the price difference and new features
  3. Confirm your upgrade
  4. Access your new features immediately
</Steps>

### Downgrade Your Plan
<Steps>
  1. Select 'Downgrade' from your subscription settings
  2. Choose your new plan
  3. Your new plan will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle
</Steps>

<Warning>
When downgrading, you'll maintain access to your current plan's features until the end of your billing period.
</Warning>

## FAQ

<Accordion title="How does billing work?">
  Billing occurs monthly or annually, depending on your chosen plan. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
  Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
  We accept all major credit cards through our secure Stripe payment processing.
</Accordion>
